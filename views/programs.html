Cards of Kindness



Our Goal

When COVID-19 hit Texas, the foster care system had to adapt under unprecedented circumstances:

  • To spread messages of hope and kindness during this difficult time

  • To share words of support for Dallas foster youth as they adapted to virtual learning and placements during social distancing

  • To show our appreciation for front-line, essential CPS employees who continued to support kids

Our Impact

  • Organized over 200 handwritten cards for CPS employees and Dallas area foster youth

  • Assembled over 200 care packages for foster youth and CPS employees

  • Delivered first iteration of Cards of Kindness on June 19th, 2020


One Night Bags



Our Goal

When youth enter CPS, they recieve only two trash bags for their belongings. These kids deserve better:

  • Replace trash bags with age-appropriate duffle bags for Dallas area foster youth

  • Equip these duffel bags with essential toiletries like underwear & socks and other comfort items

  • Incorporate products that address the hair and skin care needs of Black foster youth


Our Impact

  • Fundraised $1185 that will go towards buying One Night Bag Supplies

  • Recieved $1500 from the Worsfold Grant that will go towards buying the duffel bags

  • Current Status: Ordering One Night Bag supplies and scheduling date to organize event
