Our Mission

To support the needs of North Texas foster youth by:

  • Fundraising for service initiatives that will serve foster youth
  • Volunteering with community partners and with fellow UT Dallas students
  • Mentoring and serving as external support for foster youth coming into college
  • Advocating for the rights of foster youth and spreading awareness
